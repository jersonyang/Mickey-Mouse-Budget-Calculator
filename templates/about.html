Our Story

We are Faith, Jerson, Nooraan, and Youssra- part of FinTech Focus' 2023 Wells Fargo Session A cohort. FinTech Focus is a two-week program providing incoming college freshmen with the principles of computer science and exposure to the financial industry. FinTech Focus is sponsored by financial institutions such as Bank of America, JPMorgan Chase & Co., Morgan Stanley, and Wells Fargo.
